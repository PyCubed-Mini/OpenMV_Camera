This folder contains the final and intermediate results of calibrating the standard lens on the OpenMV camera.

Contents:
- "Calibrated Pics" has various post-calibration VGA images to demonstrate the ability of the focused lens.
- "QVGA Video Stills" has screenshots taken from the camera when it was in QVGA video mode.  These results were used to generate a starting point for VGA video calibration.
- "VGA Snapshots" contains the scale of images (from -2 to +3) used to calibrate the camera for VGA snapshots.  This is probably the one you're interested in.
- "VGA Video Stills" has screenshots taken from the camera when it was in VGA video mode.  These results were used to generate a starting point for VGA snapshot calibration.
